UNHAPPY MEDIUM

By: T.J.Brown

‘A very funny book, in the spirit of Neil Gaiman and Terry Pratchett's Good Omens, or Christopher Moore. ... a rollicking finish.’ Dr. Newton Barlow had everything he could ask for – a glittering career in the lab and on television, a beautiful wife, and best of all, the opportunity to promote his rock-solid certainty that the supernatural world is about as real as a bucket of fairies. But the cocksure Dr. Barlow is set for a tumble. Mired in accusations of fraud, Newton is disowned by the scientific establishment, ejected from the family home and thrown bodily from his ivory tower.
